Objectives:

In this game, your goal is to score points by hitting moles that pop up from holes. Each successful mole hit earns you 10 points.

Avoid Hitting Rabbits:

Avoid clicking on rabbits, as hitting them will reduce your score and end the game. The game lasts for 30 seconds.

Whack moles, avoid rabbits, and aim for the highest score.

Hit the reset button to restart the game at any point and the play/stop music button to control music. If you need a refresh on the rules, then click the instructions button.

Tips:

Stay focused, click quickly for moles, and refrain from clicking rabbits to maximize your score.
